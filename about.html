guilherme fidelis.


Born and raised in the cosmopolitan, melting pot city of São Paulo, Brazil, Guil (he/him) always had a creative and design-oriented mind. After spending time In Kyoto, Japan, and studying fashion design in university and working in a haute couture atelier, Guilherme moved to Dublin, Ireland, where his interest in body art converged with his personal artistic style to lead him into a career of tattooing.


Guil's tattooing style derives from his own artwork which he has been developing since his early teens.

the studio.


Located in Maynooth, co. Kildare, guilguri tattoo studio is a private, appointment-only studio where your tattooing session with Guil can be a relaxed, personal, and enjoyable experience. An LGBTQIA+ inclusive studio, it is a safe and welcoming space where everyone can feel comfortable throughout the tattooing process.
